# Persona Library

## Purpose

This document defines the starter portfolio of personas for the longevity MVP.

It is intentionally broader than a single MVP persona so we can reason about:

- nationality and local expectations
- age and life stage
- digital fluency
- price sensitivity and willingness to pay
- different health motivations

## Important Note

The dataset does not contain income or wealth. Affluence tiers below are service-design assumptions used to shape pricing, packaging, and communication. They must not be treated as factual patient attributes unless validated by real business inputs.

## Persona Design Principles

- Cover prevention and chronic-risk use cases
- Span younger adults through older adults
- Include both price-sensitive and premium-preference segments
- Reflect the actual country spread present in the data
- Keep the product patient-facing and action-oriented
